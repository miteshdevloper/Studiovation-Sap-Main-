About Us

Greetings Innovators,

We are so pleased that you have chosen us for your journey of innovation and creativity. We are Team SAP, and our project is called STUDIOVATION.

Our platform is a space where students and innovators can come together to create their ideas and businesses. It offers a wealth of data and news on current global happenings and issues, helping you gather information. With our crafting table, you can bring your ideas to life and build prototypes with expert support and evaluations. As economic rocket innovators, we also offer the opportunity for you to interact with others through surveys, collect investments, and execute your vision in the start-up community.

Come join us to think big, innovate, and turn your ideas into reality!
